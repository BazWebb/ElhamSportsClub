About Elham Sports Club

A friendly, welcoming sports community in the heart of Elham

Who We Are

Elham Sports Club is a community-driven club offering tennis, badminton and pickleball for all ages and abilities. Our focus is on creating a welcoming, social environment where members can stay active and enjoy sport together.

Our Facilities

🎾 Tennis Courts

Two all-weather courts, fully floodlit and available year-round until 10pm. Lighting meets LTA recommended standards.

🏸 Indoor Sports

Badminton and pickleball are played inside Elham Village Hall, offering great facilities in all weather conditions.

⚽ Multi-use Court

Court 2 is also used for 5-a-side football and basketball, making the club versatile for different activities.

Community & Social Events

We host regular social events including fun tennis days, picnics and our annual supper. The club is as much about community as it is about sport.
