Club Overview

🎾 Mike Ridley Court (Court 1)

This court is used solely for tennis and is not suitable for contact sports.

⚽ Multi-use Court

Court 2 supports tennis, pickleball, 5-a-side football and basketball.

🏸 Indoor Sports

Badminton and pickleball sessions take place inside the Village Hall. The high ceiling provides a perfect arena for these sports.

Facilities

🌟 Quality Facilities

Our courts feature modern LED floodlighting built to LTA standards. The lights can be used all year round until 10pm.

The club also provides dedicated toilets and washrooms within the Village Hall.

Community & Events

🎉 Social Events

A relaxed, friendly atmosphere focused on enjoyment and participation.
