🛡️ Safeguarding Standards

To maintain LTA registration, Elham Sports Club must meet the following safeguarding standards:

  • Safeguarding Policy & Procedures clearly communicated and implemented
  • Diversity & Inclusion Policy & Procedures clearly communicated and implemented
  • Appointment of a trained Welfare Officer
  • Relevant personnel to complete Criminal Records Checks
  • Annual Risk Assessment of the venue and facilities
  • Level 3+ coaches to hold LTA Accreditation or Accreditation+
