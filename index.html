🏸 Badminton

Badminton is a high energy sport played indoors with lightweight racquets over a high net. It is usually played by four players although singles is also popular for the more energetic.

🏓 Pickleball

Pickleball is a fast-growing paddle sport that combines elements of tennis, badminton, and table tennis, played with paddles and a perforated plastic ball on a small court with a net similar to tennis. It can be played by two or four players
