Welcome to Elham Sports Club 35

A friendly, welcoming sports club offering tennis, badminton, pickleball and social events for all ages and abilities.

About

About Us

Elham Sports Club comprises two all-weather tennis courts and badminton and pickleball facilities within Elham Village Hall. The outdoor tennis courts are floodlit and available all year round until 10pm. Court 1 is used exclusively for tennis; Court 2 is used for tennis and other games including 5-a-side football and basketball.

History

The club has been in existence since 1987 and boasts a strong membership.

Committee

The Sports Club is run by a committee that oversees membership, maintains the courts and organises fun tournaments and social events.

Safeguarding

We prioritise safety and wellbeing for all members.
