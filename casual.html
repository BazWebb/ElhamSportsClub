Weekend Availability

Court 2 is open for casual use, whether for tennis or other multi sports, at the weekend.

Booking Outside of Weekend Hours

At other times, you may hire Court 2 on a casual basis. To do so, please contact Brett by telephone on 07875 797747, or Clive on 07771 922192, or alternatively via email at stanyonc@aol.com. They will organise access to the court for you. Please note that there is a charge for casual use.

Try before you buy

If you are interested in playing and considering membership, you are welcome to attend one of our club sessions free of charge. This offers you the opportunity to experience the club atmosphere and decide if it is suitable for you. To participate in a club session, please contact Brett on 07875 797747 or Steve on 07984 763156. You will receive a warm welcome!
