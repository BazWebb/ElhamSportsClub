History & Facilities

Elham Sports Club

History

Elham tennis courts first opened in September 1987 and have since been managed by the Elham Sports Club Committee, which operates as part of the Elham Village Hall.

Over the years, the club has expanded its facilities to cater for a variety of sports and activities within the local community.

Multi-Sport Courts

Court 2 was specially adapted to accommodate both 5-a-side football and basketball, enhancing the versatility of the club’s facilities and providing members with access to a range of sporting options.

Village Hall Developments

At the close of 1999, the new Village Hall was inaugurated. The main hall was purpose-built to include a badminton court.

In 2023, pickleball was introduced, further broadening the range of sports available to members.

Membership & Activities

The club boasts a thriving membership, with regular sessions for tennis, badminton and pickleball taking place throughout the week.

Members benefit from an easy-to-use court booking system and the opportunity to arrange coaching sessions with a qualified coach.

Courts & Lighting

Both tennis court surfaces and their surroundings have recently been refurbished.

The floodlights have also been upgraded to high-quality LED lighting that meets LTA recommended standards.

Social Events

Social activities are an important aspect of club life, including fun tennis and picnic days as well as an annual supper for members to enjoy.

Club Amenities

The Sports Club provides dedicated toilets and washrooms located within the Village Hall for the convenience of members and visitors.

Club Ethos

The club is committed to promoting enjoyment and fitness in a safe and secure environment, with an emphasis on having fun whilst keeping active.
